# Web DiceBot Marketplace: Seller Guidelines

Welcome to the Web DiceBot Marketplace! To ensure a professional and transparent environment for everyone, please follow these guidelines when listing your scripts.

## 🚀 Listing Workflow

- **USDT Pricing:** All scripts are priced in **USDT** to maintain value stability.
- **Mandatory Demo Video:** You must provide a link to a demo video showing the script running on a real casino with Web DiceBot for at least **30 minutes**. Link video platforms accepted: YouTube, Vimeo, Costv, Loom, TikTok, Twitter, and more.
- **Detailed Instructions:** Provide a comprehensive guide for buyers, including setup steps, strategy details, recommended balance, and important warnings.

## 💸 Payment & Commission

- **Flexible Payments:** Buyers pay using **TRX** or **POL**. The system automatically handles the real-time conversion from your USDT price.
- **Platform Commission:** There is a **20%** platform fee to maintain the infrastructure.
- **Transparent Chain Payment:** When a sale occurs, the Seller receives **100%** of the payment directly from the Buyer. The system then automatically deducts the **20%** commission from the Seller's wallet.
- **Financial History:** Your "My Invoice" page will show a transparent breakdown for every sale:
  1.  An invoice for **+100%** revenue received.
  2.  An invoice for **-20%** platform commission.

## ⭐ Reputation System (Trust Rating)

Your reputation is your most valuable asset on the marketplace. It is calculated as follows:
**Score = (Upvotes × 5) - (Downvotes × 5) + (Successful Sales × 1)**

- **Trust Badge:** High reputation scores increase your listing visibility and buyer confidence.
- **Verified Voting:** Only users who have purchased your script can vote (Up or Down), ensuring 100% authentic feedback.

## ⚠️ Responsibilities & Rules

- Sellers are responsible for providing working scripts and clear instructions.
- Fraudulent behavior or uploading malicious code will result in a **Permanent Ban** and a reputation reset.
- Past results do not guarantee future performance; please be honest in your descriptions and strategy claims.
